## 🎯 How to Play

### Basic Rules
1. **Slice the Fruits** 🍎 - Swipe across fruits to slice them
2. **Avoid Bombs** 💣 - Don't touch the black bombs or you'll lose a life
3. **Score Points** 📊 - Each fruit gives you points
4. **Achieve Combos** 🔥 - Slice multiple fruits in one swipe for bonus points

### Scoring System
- **Single Fruit**: 10 points
- **Double Slice**: 25 points (2x combo)
- **Triple Slice**: 50 points (3x combo)
- **Quad Slice**: 100 points (4x combo)
- **Penta Slice**: 200 points (5x combo!)

### Power-ups
- **🍇 Grape Bunch**: Extra points when sliced
- **🥥 Coconut**: Harder to slice but worth more points
- **🌟 Star Fruit**: Temporary slow motion effect
- **💎 Diamond Fruit**: Rare appearance, massive points!

## 💡 Pro Tips & Tricks

### 🎯 Accuracy Tips
- **Wait for the right moment** - Don't rush, timing is everything
- **Watch the trajectory** - Predict where fruits will be
- **Use both hands** - On mobile, use multiple fingers for better coverage

### 🔥 Combo Strategies
- **Group slicing** - Wait for fruits to cluster together
- **Chain reactions** - Some fruits explode and help slice nearby fruits
- **Power-up timing** - Save power-ups for maximum combo potential

### 🛡️ Survival Tactics
- **Stay calm** - Panic leads to mistakes
- **Prioritize bombs** - Always avoid bombs first
- **Edge slicing** - Slice from the edges to avoid accidental bomb hits
